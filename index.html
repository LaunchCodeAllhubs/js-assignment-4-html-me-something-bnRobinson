Seven Shades of Brittany

By: Brittany Robinson

Red Like...

Positive Connotations

  • Love
  • Passion
  • Strength
  • Sacrifice

Cheeks burning, heart pounding, awkward first kisses. Shiny plastic solo cups on a beach made for two. Red is the color of love, and the feeling of my heartbeat when I think of you. Plus signs on a little white stick, stretchy dresses and stretchy-er skin. Flushed from hot flashes and anticipation. Red is the color of excitement, eagerly waiting to start our life.

Orange Like...

Positive Orange Connotations

  • Energy
  • Youth
  • Optimism
  • Enthusiasm

Shiny tassels on a May afternoon, gleaming with hope. Gold lettering on a black document holder. Orange is energy, creativity, and waiting to take the world by storm. It's the color of the sunset every graduate rides off into.

Yellow Like...

Yellow Positive Connotations

  • Happiness
  • Intelligence
  • Optimism
  • Positivity

Hot air balloons drifting out of a clear blue sky. Yellow is happiness, and sunflowers, and my grandma telling me I'm her sunshine.

Green Like...

Green Positive Connotations

  • Growth
  • Renewal
  • Prosperity
  • Luck

Blue Like...

Blue Positive Connotations

  • Loyalty
  • Calmness
  • Trust
  • Responsibility

Indigo Like...

Indigo Positive Connotations

  • Intuition
  • Order
  • Mystery
  • Serenity

The smell of rain and an electric hum, the wind picks up just a bit. An incoming storm or a morning sunrise both hold a world of their own. Indigo is the color of mystery and serenity- the peace that comes with accepting the unknown.

Violet Like...

Violet Positive Connotations

  • Royalty
  • Luxury
  • Dignity
  • Peace

Chubby baby cheeks, sweet, slobbery kisses, that newborn smell- Violet will never mean purple again. Violet is sweet and sassy, playful and mischevious, ready to take the world in her tiny baby fists. Violet means sleepless nights and morning cuddles, getting your hair pulled and laughing about it. Violet is the peace and luxury and dignity of getting to be your mom.
