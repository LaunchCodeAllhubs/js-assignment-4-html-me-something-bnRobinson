Seven Shades of Brittany

By: Brittany Robinson

Red Like...

Positive Connotations

  • Love
  • Passion
  • Strength
  • Sacrifice

Cheeks burning, heart pounding, awkward first kisses. Shiny plastic solo cups on a beach made for two. Red is the color of love, and the feeling of my heartbeat when I think of you. Plus signs on a little white stick, stretchy dresses and stretchy-er skin. Flushed skin from hot flashes and excitment. Red is the color of excitement, eagerly waiting to start our life.

Negative Connotations

  • Anger
  • Danger
  • Revenge
  • Violence

A river of brake lights when you're running late. Red is also the color of frustration as I try not to become another St.Louis road rage story. Why do the bad drivers only come out when I'm running late?

Orange Like...

Positive Orange Connotations

  • Energy
  • Youth
  • Optimism
  • Enthusiasm

Shiny tassels on a May afternoon, gleaming with hope. Gold lettering on a black document holder. Orange is energy, creativity, and waiting to take the world by storm. It's the color of the sunset every graduate rides off into.

Negative Orange Conotations

  • Superficial
  • Impatient
  • Exhibitionism
  • Over the Top

Broken pencils lying on the floor. Late autumn sun streaming through a dusty window, signaling that it's past time to go home. Orange is determination, even to your detriment.

Yellow Like...

Yellow Positive Connotations

  • Happiness
  • Intelligence
  • Optimism
  • Positivity

Hot air balloons drifting out of a clear blue sky. Yellow is happiness, and sunflowers, and my grandma telling me I'm her sunshine.

Yellow Negative Connotations

  • Cowardice
  • Betrayal
  • Sickness
  • Caution

A foreign substance exiting your body... from both ends. Does your body care if it's your 21st birthday? Nope, food poisoning waits for no one.
